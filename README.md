#Commuter Theme For Text Editors

##Vasoline for your eyes without the smudge.
Crafted to be suitable when commuting by train. After years of testing works equally good in dark/light situations (no train travelling needed).

###Supported Editors (Current versions)


- Textmate – Commuter.tmTheme
- Sublime Text – Commuter.tmTheme
- Xcode – Commuter.dvtcolortheme
